Our Story

In 1940, Mario and Gracia Diaz opened a little three-table cafe and bakery for early-rising farmers and workers at San Antonio’s Mercado. Sixty years later Nana's Bakery is a world-famous landmark - the place hometown regulars and hungry tourists go for authentic Mexican Bread, quality European and American bread and pastries, and a good cup of coffee. Our bakery is a signature of downtown San Antonio, TX. Mario's and Gracia's children and grandchildren continue the family tradition of good baked goods and big-hearted hospitality, it is still located in Market Square, El Mercado.

As the family grew our bakery grew, and a second location was opened in 1973 by Mario's and Gracia's daughter Lupita. They brought to College Station, the sames recipes and customer service. In addition, Lupita attended the prestigious Auguste Escoffier School of Culinary Arts, and graduated with honors earning a scholarship to study in Paris, France.

Chef Lupita Diaz has earned many honors, among them the Michelin Star; however she decided to come back home and continue with the family tradition. She wants to preserve the traditional recipes, and teach the new generations. You can be part of our kitchen by applying for an internship at Nana's Bakery.

Contact Us

We are at your service in our two locations. Nana's Bakery at Market Square, El Mercado in downtown San Antonio, Texas; and Nana's Bakery on 505 University Dr. in College Station, Texas.

Our mission is to serve you, there is no order too big, and there is no order to small. You can come over to our bakery for a freshly baked cookie and cup of coffee, or order a wedding Cake and a pastry buffets for 500 guests.

Baking is our passion and our tradition. Chef Nana Lupita and her children love how food brings people together, and they are always ready for a talk at the coffee table.

Come join us! There is always space at Nana Lupita's table.
